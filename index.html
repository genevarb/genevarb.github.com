    Geneva.rb - Geneva Ruby Brigade

    We're a bunch of Ruby programmers based in Geneva, Switzerland. We get together every month for presentations, demos and discussions.

    Next meet-up: Tuesday, August 14th 2012 at Université de Genève from 7 to 10pm.

    We share knowledge on the first or second tuesday of each month about the Ruby programming language.
    You aren't expected to be an expert to join us! Meetings are free and open to the public.
